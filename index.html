Main Ingredients

  • 2 Jalapeno Peppers
  • 5 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 6 Strips of Raw Bacon
  • 1.5 Teaspoons Black Pepper
  • 2 Teaspoons of Salt
  • 1 Tablespoon of Olive Oil
  • 1Lb Medium Shell Pasta
  • 8 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 3 Tablespoons of Flour
  • 1 Tablspoon of Powdered Mustard
  • 1.25 Cups White Cheddar
  • 1.25 Cups Sarp Ceddar
  • 1.25 Cups Parmesan
  • 1.25 Cups Monterey Jack
  • 1.25 Cups Asiago
  • 2 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 2 Teaspoons Thyme
  • 1 Teaspoons Paprika
  • 1 Egg
  • 1.5 Teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 Cups Bread Crumbs


Supplies

  • Chef Knife/Slap Chop
  • Tongs
  • Paper Towels
  • aluminum-foil
  • Measuring Spoons (tsp-cup)
  • Food Processor/ Cheese Grater(if cheese not already shredded)
  • Whisk
  • Oven/Stove top
  • Large Cutting Board
  • Large Saute Pan
  • Small Plates
  • Large Pot
  • Large Mixing Bowl
  • Colander
  • Baking Dish

Steps

  1. Take large cutting board and Chef Knife/ Slap Chop finely dice 2 Jalapeno, 5 Garlic Cloves, 1 Red Bell Pepper, 1 Green Bell Pepper, and 1 Yellow Onion. Set aside. approximate cooking time (1-5 Minutes).
  2. Pre-heat Large Saute Panon the Stove Top at medium heat. approximate cooking time if using Stove linke above(2-4 Minutes)
  3. Place 10 strips of bacon in the pan, & season them lightly with black pepper (~0.25 tsp on each side).
  4. Cook at medium heat until the bacon is crispy. Regularly flip the bacon & rotate strips between the center & edges of the pan.(approximate cooking time 3-5 Minutes)
  5. Cover a small plate with a folded paper towel. With tongs, set the bacon strips on the towel-covered plate.
  6. Cover the bacon with a folded paper towel, & pat dry (carefully...do not touch hot oil).
  7. Drain all but 1 Tbsp of the bacon grease from the large saute pan into an aluminum foil bowl.
  8. Add the diced fruits & vegetables from Step 1 into the large saute pan.
  9. Cook at medium heat until the onion begins to carmelize. Remove from heat. approximate cook time (9 Minutes)
  10. Chop the fried bacon into 1/4" chunks.
  11. Fill pot with water until the water level is 2-3 inches away from the rim of the pot. Add 1 tsp salt
  12. Bring to a boil on high heat. approximate cook time (4 minutes)
  13. Add 1 lb medium shell pasta, and boil on high heat until al dente. approximate cook time (9 Minutes)
  14. if not pre-shredded. Shred the cheese and mix it together. approximate cook time (10 minutes)
  15. Strain the pasta.
  16. In a large stock pot Whisk together 1/4 cup water, 1/2 cup flour, and 1 tbsp powdered mustard.
  17. Cook 1 minute on medium heat.
  18. Add 2 cups heavy whipping cream, 2 cups milk, 1 bay leaf, 0.5 tsp thyme, & 1 tsp paprika.
    19. Heat the mixture on medium heat until it is just below a boil.
  19. Preheat the oven to 375° F.
  20. With tongs, remove & discard the bay leaf.
  21. Mix 1 egg with 1 tbsp of the mixture from the large stock pot. Then mix in 1 more tbsp.
  22. Slowly whisk in 3/4 of the shredded cheese, then the egg mixture.
  23. Stir in 1 tsp black pepper, 1 tsp cayenne pepper, 1 tsp salt, the fried fruits & vegetables, & 2/3 of the chopped fried bacon.
  24. Sitr in the strained pasta from Step 15.
  25. Pour in the mixture from the large sauce pan, then cover with the remaining shredded cheese.
  26. Melt 4 tbsp butter on medium heat.
  27. Cover with the bread crumb mixture.
  28. Cover with a glass lid or foil
  29. Bake in the oven at 375° F for 30 minutes.
  30. Serve
